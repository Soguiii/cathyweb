About Cathy

A small page built with care, in her memory.

Cathy Portillo

This website is a tribute to our beloved Cathy Portillo — a person whose quiet presence left a lasting impression on everyone around her.

Through the Mood feature, we invite you to connect with her expressions and feel her energy in small, meaningful ways. Each mood reflects a side of Cathy that those who knew her will recognize and cherish.

Her photos, her spirit, and her story will continue to live here — for those who miss her, and for those who want to know her.

"Until then, Cathy — we keep you close in the only ways we know how."
